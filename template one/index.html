About

Less is more work

SAAED robot, A robot that was invented to help in disasters. It is a robot that explores and locates people who are stranded or affected. At that time, it collects information such as the temperature, the intensity of the flame spread, if any, and the proportion of gases in the area. Upon learning of this information from the rescuers, it is taken into account the tools that need to be taken to the affected person, thus facilitating the rescuer's access to the person.

And if the robot is supported and adopted later, it will be transformed into a rescue robot without the help of rescuers, as microcontrollers will be introduced to it that take into account the work and application of artificial intelligence by more than 60%.
